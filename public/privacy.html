Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

This policy explains how Getani ("we", "our") collects and uses your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • Generated prompts and image metadata
  • Usage activity (credit usage, plan selection)
  • IP address and browser/device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Track and enforce credit usage
  • Improve service performance and security
  • Send occasional product or billing updates (if opted-in)

3. Data Storage and Processing

Your data is stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). We take steps to secure your data and only retain necessary records for service functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited data with trusted services like:

  • Firebase Authentication (user login)
  • Firestore (credit tracking)
  • Stripe (for billing if applicable)

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Email us at contact@getani.net for any requests.

6. Cookies

We use cookies for login sessions and analytics. By using the site, you consent to the use of cookies.

7. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We’ll notify you via the site if changes are significant.
